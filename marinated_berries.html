Marinated Berries

Description

Freshly picked berries, soaked in a blend of lemon juice and vanilla extract. What a heavenly tasting temptation for your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 1 dl water
  • 50g sugar
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 600g mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, raspberries)
  • 2 twigs of fresh peppermint

Steps

  1. Juice the lemon
  2. Cut the vanilla pod in half lengthwise and scrape out the beads
  3. Add vanilla pod, beads, water, and lemon juice to a small saucepan
  4. Crush cardamom pods and add to saucepan. Bring to a boil
  5. Simmer the mixture for 2 minutes until it reaches the consistency of syrup
  6. Pour mixture into bowl, add the berries and mix everything well
  7. Cut the peppermint leaves thinly and sprinkle over the berries
