Toblerone Mousse

Description

Follow these instructions to create your own chocolate mousse, using the finest Swiss "Toblerone" chocolate. Try it, it is simpler than you might think and well-worth the effort.

I have made this recipe many times years ago when I lived in Switzerland. Now, I am happy to share it with my friends and family here in the US.

Ingredients

  • 150g dark Toblerone chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 T powdered sugar
  • 2 dl heavy cream

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pot of boiling water
  2. Be careful that no water touches the chocolate
  3. Mix the melted chocolate with a fork until creamy and smooth
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the egg and powdered sugar until lighter in color
  5. Add the melted chocolate and stir until combined
  6. Beat the heavy cream until stiff and fold under the egg and chocolate mixture
  7. Cover and store in the refrigerator for 3 hours until firm
