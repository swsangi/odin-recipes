Lemon Sorbet

Description

This is what summer looks like! This refreshing lemon sorbet with elderberry syrup is a big hit for everyone.

Ingredients

  • 4 lemons
  • 2dl water
  • 75g powdered sugar
  • 1/2dl elderberry syrup
  • 2 fresh egg whites
  • 1 dash of salt
  • 25g powdered sugar

  1. Add 75g of powdered sugar, syrup, juice from 4 lemons, zest from 2 lemons to a metal mixing bowl
  2. Mix well, cover, and put into freezer for 1 hour
  3. Meanwhile, beat egg whites and salt until stiff. Add 25g powdered sugar
  4. Fold egg mixture carefully into the slightly frozen lemon water
  5. Cover, and put back into the freezer for about 3 more hours. Stir carefully 2 or 3 times while the sorbet is freezing
